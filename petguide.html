Pet Guide

Here are some useful pet quide tips for your pets. Easy and convenient for you to learn more on how to understand them. basic intructions to useful information not just for your pet but also for other animals.

  • Pet Training Guides

    Train behaviors in small steps so your pet doesn't fail too often and become frustrated. Give your pet a lot of attention and a variety of rewards for each correct response. Only reinforce a behavior you want your pet to repeat; don't call attention to incorrect behavior.

  • Make Them Behave

    Decide what behaviors to train your pet to perform by watching your pet's everyday activities. Give him praise when he brings a toy back to you. The next time he retrieves, place an object in his path that he has to jump over to reach you then immediately reward him for jumping. Keep reinforcement consistent.

  • Food That They Need

    There are seven classes of nutrients that are absolutely essential to the growing, breeding and eating of animals. These nutrients are: carbohydrates, fats, fibre, minerals, proteins, vitamins and water. Proteins and fats provide structural material to build muscle and help the animal grow.

  • Do's and Don'ts

    DO research pet foods.
    DO give your pet plenty of water.
    DON'T overfeed your pet.
    DO use supplements.
    DON'T bathe your pet too often.
    DO treat your pet for fleas and ticks.
    DO know your pet !!

  • Pet Foods

    Well-balanced food contains nutrients that keep your pet's coat healthy and shiny, strengthen the immune system, keep the digestive system working properly, that can help your pet maintain a healthier lifestyle.

  • Healthy Recipes for Pets

    Kale, Carrots, Pumpkins, Sweet Potatoes, Fish, Nori (dried seaweed), Chia seeds, Quinoa, Yogurt, Blueberries, Sweet Potato, Mushrooms, Peanut Butter, Honey, Banana, Cooked beef, and small amounts of lean deli meats.
